Welcome to Best Exotic TOEFL


The biggest bang for your buck in online TOEFL resources.








Some of our best experiences with learning have been with tutors. Oftentimes "on-site" in their rooftops of their homes, sometimes during free lessons in a vacant classroom, always on our time and never their own. Their instruction was "personalized" in the very human sense of the word.

Our teachers have high impact, but low visibility; perhaps the underlying character trait is humility. Despite having years of wisdom and practiced patience in dealing with all kinds of students, these tutors are systematically disadvantaged by scale, especially if they transition online. Large providers, large enough to espouse selection bias, can outmarket and outmaneuver these small scale tutors.

Some of our best experiences with learning, over tea, biscuits, and repartee with Girish Uncle (recently graduating from Sir), deserve to be recognized and supported, not outmoded. Our product is an attempt to do just that.
